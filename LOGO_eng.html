BIME LOGO

  1. This design useschromosomes , gears、和, and electronic signals as a combination to represent the integration of biology, industry, and electromechanical engineering.
  2. The word BIME appears in the signal, which is the abbreviation of "Bio-industrial Mechatronics Engineering", giving people a digital and electronic feeling。
  3. "Technology always comes from human nature", with chromosomes as the design, it is expected that the research and development will take practical application as the consideration, and meet the global well-being.
    4. Green represents thriving business and infinite vitality; blue represents the blue sky and ocean, inclusive of all things; and the gradient from the green of chromosomes to the blue of gears represents the biological industry and the combination of mechanical and electrical also represents the connection between science and humanities. Apart from reading, it still retains vitality and enthusiasm.
  4. The inverted triangle in the chromosome represents the miniature of the "National Chung Hsing University" emblem. It also means that the department cultivates talents integrating technology on campus. In the future, it will lead to industrial upgrading, breakthroughs and innovations, allowing Taiwan to be under the impact of WTO and other countries in the world. still have a place.
